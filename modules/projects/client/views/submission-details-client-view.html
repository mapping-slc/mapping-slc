Submission Details

Mapping Salt Lake City invites everyone to add their voices to the mix.

Please also include all necessary personal or archival photos to accompany your entry. All entries should be submitted to our editors via our online Project Submission form.

We review all entries for Mapping Salt Lake City before publishing them live on the site. We accept reprints, so long as you own the copyright. Sadly, we cannot yet pay for material.

If you'd like some ideas for projects

  • Oral histories or interviews with Salt Lake City residents
  • Essays, including video, multimedia, graphic (comic-book), photo, and audio/sound essays
  • Essays on space and place, including environment and architecture
  • Essays on immigration, race, religion, politics or culture as it relates to Salt Lake City
  • Personal or historical maps made with any interactive mapping technology (we may be able to provide limited assistance with the technology. Please contact us for more info.)
  • Walking tours
  • Personal essays that specifically relate to Salt Lake City
  • Essays/interviews/projects about the city's art/music/creative scene
  • Profiles of people that are or who have been active in changing the city

We are also looking for more entries to our This Was Here archive.
